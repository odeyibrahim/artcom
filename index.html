Ulõmeans Home

Nestled in vibrant Lekki Phase 1,
Ulõ Hotel is the epitome of modern luxury
and intimate hospitality.
A tranquil escape for the discerning traveller. Every room is thoughtfully designed
to exude charm and elegance.
Contemporary furnishings, plush bedding,
and cutting-edge amenities —
high-speed Wi‑Fi, smart TVs, and more.
Strategically located in the heart of Lekki Phase 1,
we offer easy access to the Lekki Conservation Centre,
Admiralty Way's finest restaurants,
and the city's best entertainment. Choose Ulõ for an unforgettable stay,
where boutique luxury meets personalised hospitality.
Ideal for couples, solo travellers, and small groups —
a serene retreat amid the lively energy of Lagos.
